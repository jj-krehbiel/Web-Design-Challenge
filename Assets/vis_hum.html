Humidity vs. Lattituted


My data does not support any significant correlation between humidity and Lattitude. You can see the scatter points are pretty randomly spread out across the plot.

Although my own un-scientific annecdotal evidence tells me that humidity is much worse in tropical climates closer to the equator, this does not pan out in the data. Perhaps I was simply more aware of the humidity in those environments due to the increased temperature as well. Either way, it illustrates that we cannot always trust our own personal experiences when confronted with quantitative data.
